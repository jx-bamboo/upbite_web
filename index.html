Introduction

The Upbite Alliance is an organization that connects digital asset exchanges around the world, aiming to seamlessly aggregate the overall liquidity and depth of global digital assets and build a network of weakly centralized alliances.In August 2019, the upbite alliance was formally established,and consensus has been reached on three exchanges across regions, cultures and time. The first one is the WOEx exchange, who has obtained the US msb license, which is issued and supervised by the US Treasury; the second one is the European exchange Alt-coin, which is licensed by the Estonian government; and the third one is the Australian agdaex exchange, has obtained the Australian government license.Upbite is currently actively expanding the scope of the alliance, and it is expected that two licensed exchanges will join the alliance at the end of the year.

Advantage

Become a member of the Upbite Alliance, which is integrated into the Upbite global industrial cluster and can be used throughout the entire ecosystem through WTA Eco-Certification: Exchange Alliance Ecology, Upbite DApp Ecology, Blockchain Ecology Research Center, Mine Pool Ecology, Industry Incubation, Digital Asset Management, Public interest and community. Including the global exchange voting rights of the Union Exchange, global alliance dividends, and so on. All members of the Upbite Alliance share the depth of the exchange, open and interoperate with the platform resources, and create an ecological environment in which exchanges help each other, resulting in the free circulation of digital characters and the realization of a win-win situation for exchanges and users.

WTA Introduction

The Upbite Exchange Alliance Eco-Certification, referred to as “WTA”, was issued jointly by the exchanges in the Upbite Alliance, with a total amount of 2.979 billion, and guaranteed never to be issued. WTA can realize free conversion and circulation within the alliance, share the depth of trading on all major platforms, and more effectively provide sufficient transaction liquidity, and transactions can be circulated in a timely and efficient manner. WTA will be given more usage scenarios, such as community incentives, fee deductions, community node rewards, and more. We look forward to and welcome more partners to join the Upbite Alliance in the future.
